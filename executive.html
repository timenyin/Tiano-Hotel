Executive Room

Executive Business Room

This elegantly designed Executive Room offers a blend of luxury, comfort, and cutting-edge technology for a superior guest experience.

The room features a large Smart TV with access to streaming services and satellite channels for entertainment and information. Entry into the room is through a secure access-control door system, allowing keycard or biometric entry for enhanced privacy and safety.

A professionally installed beauty light system provides adjustable lighting for makeup, grooming, and photography, ensuring perfect illumination at all times.

The bed is an auto-pedi and semi-autopedic luxury bed, designed to support proper posture and body alignment, with automated comfort adjustments for restful sleep.

The bathroom is fitted with a kinetic-energy powered smart shower, which automatically regulates water flow and temperature using motion and pressure control technology — promoting energy efficiency and comfort.

For ultimate safety, the room is secured with bullet-resistant reinforced doors, offering a high level of protection and peace of mind for high-profile guests.
